Controlling a device over internet can be done using two methods, ngrok and telebit. Unfortunately ngrok requires a credit card to be put in even for the free version. but ngrok is much easier and does not cause much issue.

> Using telebit:
	> From macbook type ~/telebit ssh root@blue-emu-80.telebit.io for connecting with bananapi.

> Using ngrok:
	> ssh threadbridge2@0.tcp.in.ngrok.io -p 15947
